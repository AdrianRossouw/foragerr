The product

A Sonarr-shaped manager for the comics you own.

foragerr imports and renames a DRM-free collection, matches it against ComicVine, tracks what you have and what is missing, fills the gaps through your own tooling, and serves it to any OPDS reader. Self-hosted. Nothing phones home.
